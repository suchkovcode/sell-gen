Reasons for the
low performance
of your advertising

If you work on your own:

  • You run no more than 8-10 creatives
  • You don't create new campaigns every 5-7 days
  • You use monotonous creatives
  • You use template ads
  • Your advertisements do not reflect your uniqueness
  • Your retargeting is not working properly
  • You don't work with different groups of audiences
  • You are not flexible with you ads

If you work with an agency

  • The time they spend on working with you is not enough, as they as they work with many clients
  • The agency provides many services – they are not specialists in one area
  • They don't use new promotion strategies
  • They don't do enough – they are not looking for new breakthrough solutions
  • They use the same strategies for most clients
  • They don't care about your business as if it were their own
  • They take money for adspend - not for performance

Your advertising
has problems if:

  • The payback becomes worse, even with the budget increased. The ROI of your advertisement is less than 240%
  • Your sales do not increase by 10-40% each month
  • A customer makes only 1-2 purchases from you
  • The cost of purchase does not decrease every week
  • You have products that have not been on sale for a long time
  • Advertising only recoups by offering discounts

Does this sound familiar?

Advertising without the right strategies is like a shop without products (just as empty and pointless).
